Turning LLG+ into a E-commerce Brand

Live Long Genetics (LLG+), an anti-ageing supplement, partnered with Cloud9 to transform their idea into a market-ready e-commerce brand. With no online presence, brand identity, or sales infrastructure, LLG+ needed a comprehensive strategy to launch successfully by 14th February.

Cloud9 handled every aspect, from branding and website development to social media management, advertising, and sales strategy. We crafted a distinct brand identity, developed an SEO-optimised e-commerce website, and executed targeted marketing campaigns to build awareness and drive traffic.

Physical Business Case Study

Ice 8 - An Italian gellato brand

When ICE8, an Italian-based ice cream parlour, decided to bring its delicious desserts to Pakistan, they needed more than just a plan—they needed a partner who could handle every detail of their launch and growth. That’s where CLOUD9 stepped in.

From selecting the perfect locations and crafting a standout brand identity to managing high-profile launch events and creating targeted social media campaigns, CLOUD9 became ICE8PK’s trusted growth partner. Our team provided expert consultancy, streamlined operations, and executed innovative marketing strategies, resulting in three successful launches across Rawalpindi, DHA, and F7 Jinnah Super Market.

With CLOUD9’s support, ICE8PK didn’t just enter the Pakistani market—they made a splash, setting the stage for future expansion and growth.
